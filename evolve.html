Ongoing Advisory & Improvement

Light-touch, ongoing support so your security posture keeps up with new products, markets and vendors — without needing a full-time hire.

What ongoing support can include

  • Quarterly posture reviews and roadmap refresh.
  • Security input on new products, vendors and integrations.
  • Support during incidents or “near misses”.
  • Awareness sessions for staff and leadership.
  • Preparation for audits or customer security questionnaires.

Retainer or ad-hoc, depending on your needs

How we work together over time

  • We agree a sensible cadence (e.g. monthly or quarterly).
  • You get a consistent point of contact who understands your environment.
  • We keep a living view of priorities and trade-offs as things change.
  • You can lean on me as a fractional security lead when needed.

Why ongoing, not one-and-done

Security is never “finished”, but it also doesn’t have to be overwhelming. A lightweight, regular rhythm of review and improvement lets you respond to change without constant firefighting or big, disruptive projects.

Next steps

If you already have a roadmap or have done an assessment recently, we can start from there. If not, we can combine this service with an initial posture assessment to set a clear baseline.
