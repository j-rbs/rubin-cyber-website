Ongoing Advisory & Improvement

Light-touch, ongoing support so your security posture keeps pace with new products, vendors and markets — without needing a full-time security hire.

Why ongoing support matters

Security is never “finished”, but it also doesn’t need to dominate your attention. An ongoing advisory arrangement gives you a consistent security brain to lean on, so you can make decisions confidently as things change.

What ongoing support can include

  • Quarterly posture reviews and roadmap refresh.
  • Security input on new products, integrations and vendors.
  • Support during incidents or “near misses” — including lessons learned.
  • Awareness sessions or short workshops for teams and leadership.
  • Help with customer security questionnaires or due diligence requests.

Retainer or ad-hoc, depending on your needs and cadence.

How we work together over time

  • We agree a sensible rhythm (for example, monthly or quarterly check-ins).
  • You get a single, consistent point of contact who knows your environment.
  • We keep a living view of priorities, risks and trade-offs as the business evolves.
  • You decide when to pull me in for projects, reviews or planning sessions.

If you haven’t done an assessment yet

If you don’t yet have a clear view of your current posture, we can start with a Cybersecurity Posture Assessment & Roadmap and then move into ongoing advisory. If you’ve already done an assessment elsewhere, we can pick up from that and adapt it to how you work now.

The aim is to stay practical, predictable and aligned with your actual goals.
