Foundations Implementation

Practical rollout of the basics: strong identity, protected devices, reliable backups and visibility when something goes wrong.

Typical focus areas

  • MFA & SSO, including admin and high-risk accounts.
  • Access hygiene: least privilege, joiners/movers/leavers, shared accounts.
  • Endpoint protection: hardening and monitoring for laptops and desktops.
  • Backups: making sure what you have is tested and restorable.
  • Logging and alerts so incidents don’t go unnoticed.

Milestone based · typically 3–8 weeks

How we structure the work

  • Break down the roadmap into small, clear milestones.
  • Agree who does what: your team, me, and any vendors.
  • Document simple runbooks so changes are repeatable.
  • Keep leadership updated in plain language as progress is made.

Working with your existing stack

This isn’t about ripping everything out and starting again. We start from what you already have and improve configuration, coverage and usage. Where new tools are needed, they’re chosen for fit, not just buzzwords.

What success looks like

  • Critical accounts are protected with strong, enforced MFA.
  • Access is more predictable and easier to review.
  • Devices are hardened and monitored to a sensible baseline.
  • Backups are tested and you know who does what in an incident.
