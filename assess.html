Cybersecurity Posture Assessment & Roadmap

A focused review of where you stand today — and a realistic roadmap for the next 6–12 months.

What this engagement covers

  • Governance, roles and how decisions about risk are made.
  • Identity & access: accounts, MFA, privileged access and hygiene.
  • Data: what you hold, where it lives, and how it’s protected.
  • Network & infrastructure at a practical, risk-based level.
  • Awareness, training, and how people are supported to do the right thing.

Fixed fee · typically ~2 weeks from kickoff

What you walk away with

  • A clear, non-jargony summary of your current posture.
  • A risk register in plain language, tied to business impact.
  • An asset & data inventory you can actually maintain.
  • A prioritised roadmap of improvements, grouped by effort and impact.
  • Time with me to walk through findings and answer questions.

Who this is for

  • Growing businesses that have “done the basics” but want a clearer picture.
  • Leaders preparing for audits, certifications or entering new markets.
  • Teams who want security to support growth, not slow it down.

How we work together

  • Kickoff session to define scope, systems and priorities.
  • Targeted review of documentation, configurations and processes.
  • Follow-up session to walk through the report and roadmap.
  • Optional check-ins to support you as you implement changes.
